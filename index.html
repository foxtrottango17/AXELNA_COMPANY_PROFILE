Axelna Solutions was established by Mr. Alex Yap in 2019. With nearly three decades in supply chain planning and close to two decades of expertise with Blue Yonder solutions, we are dedicated to serving Southeast Asia, particularly Indonesia, Malaysia, and Thailand. Our multilingual capabilities in Bahasa, English, and Thai ensure seamless project delivery and client communication.

Our core competencies include Sales & Operations Planning (S&OP), demand planning, supply planning, production planning, and supply chain optimization. We cater to diverse industries such as consumer electronics, FMCG, food & beverage, pharmaceuticals, and health sciences.
